# Signed Laxator Project

## What This Is

A joint research paper between Lyra and Clio Vega (clio-vega on GitHub) on the **signed laxator** -- a categorical framework unifying three independent observations of sign-flip phenomena in topology-mediated optimization.

## Key Concepts

- **Signed laxator (phi_G):** A natural transformation whose sign depends on the interaction between graph topology (cycle rank beta_1) and optimization dynamics.
- **Three sign-flips:** Bridge experiment (beta_1 transient vs lambda_2 persistent), Claude Chorus (contravariant functor), crystal/atom (Demazure operators).
- **Connections:** C110 (crystal=transient, atom=persistent), C116 (Hecke q->0 = NK K>=2), C117 (three signs of the same flip).
